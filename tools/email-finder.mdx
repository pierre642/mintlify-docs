The Email Finder bridges the gap that generic prospecting tools like Hunter.io or Dropcontact leave open: professional email addresses for French public sector decision-makers. These contacts — Directors General of Services (DGS), IT directors (DSI), procurement officers, and elected officials — follow institutional email formats that commercial databases rarely index. Skult's Email Finder queries more than 10,000 public sources to locate and verify the right address, and it does so in compliance with RGPD.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the target's LinkedIn profile">
    Navigate to the LinkedIn profile of the public sector decision-maker you want to reach. Copy the full profile URL from your browser's address bar (it will look like `https://www.linkedin.com/in/firstname-lastname-xxxxx`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the URL into the tool">
    Open [outils.skult.ai/email-finder](https://outils.skult.ai/email-finder) and paste the LinkedIn URL into the input field. No additional information is required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for enrichment to complete">
    The tool cross-references more than 10,000 public sources — official institutional directories, municipal websites, public open data — to locate and verify the email address. Most lookups complete within a few minutes; the process can take up to 5 minutes.

    <Warning>
      Enrichment time varies depending on the availability and complexity of public data for that individual. In some cases the process can take **up to 5 minutes**. Keep the browser tab open and do not refresh the page while the search is running.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the verified email address">
    Once the enrichment completes, you receive a verified professional email address that is active and up to date. Copy it directly into your email client or CRM to start your outreach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why standard tools don't cover public sector

In the private sector, tools like Hunter.io or Dropcontact work well because companies publish their contact information in ways that are easy to index. Public sector decision-makers are different: their emails follow institution-specific formats (e.g. `p.dupont@mairie-bordeaux.fr`, `pierre.dupont@departement33.fr`) that vary by entity type and are spread across thousands of individual institutional websites. Skult's Email Finder was built specifically for this landscape, drawing on over 10,000 public sources to achieve national coverage across every level of the French public sector.

<Note>
  The Email Finder is fully **RGPD compliant**. It only surfaces professional contact information published in public institutional sources. It does not process, store, or share any personal data beyond what is already publicly available.
</Note>
