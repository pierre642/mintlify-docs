Skult's free tools give you instant access to French public procurement intelligence — no account, no credit card, and no strings attached. Whether you're researching a prospect, sizing up a competitor, hunting for contracts about to expire, or stress-testing a tender document before you commit to a response, each tool below is purpose-built for B2G sales teams who sell to the French public sector.

<Note>
  All five tools are completely free and require no account or credit card. They are available at [outils.skult.ai](https://outils.skult.ai) and are fully RGPD compliant.
</Note>

## The five tools

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Procurement Explorer"
    icon="magnifying-glass-chart"
    href="/tools/procurement-explorer"
  >
    Search any French public buyer or company by name, SIREN, or SIRET to explore their full contract history, spending trends, and active sectors — sourced in real time from the official DECP dataset on data.gouv.fr.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Email Finder"
    icon="envelope-open-text"
    href="/tools/email-finder"
  >
    Enter a LinkedIn URL and get a verified professional email address for any French public sector decision-maker — DGS, DSI, buyers, or elected officials — in under 5 minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Contract Radar"
    icon="radar"
    href="/tools/contract-radar"
  >
    Identify public contracts expiring in the next 6, 9, or 12 months so you can approach the buyer before the tender is even drafted — filtered by keyword, CPV code, or department.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Competitor Detector"
    icon="binoculars"
    href="/tools/competitor-detector"
  >
    Discover every public contract won by any company — buyers, amounts, dates, and sectors — and export a full CSV to feed directly into your CRM for targeted prospecting campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Go/No-Go Analysis"
    icon="scale-balanced"
    href="/tools/go-no-go"
  >
    Upload a CCTP or RC document and receive an AI-powered Go/No-Go recommendation in under a minute — seven favoritism indicators checked entirely inside your browser, so your files never leave your device.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What powers the data

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="DECP — data.gouv.fr" icon="database">
    The Procurement Explorer, Contract Radar, and Competitor Detector all draw from the Données Essentielles de la Commande Publique (DECP) — approximately **940,000 contracts** published on data.gouv.fr, updated in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="10,000+ public sources" icon="globe">
    The Email Finder cross-references more than **10,000 public sources** — institutional directories, official websites, and open datasets — to locate and verify professional email addresses across the entire French public sector.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
