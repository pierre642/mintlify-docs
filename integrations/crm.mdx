Skult connects to your CRM so that every buying signal and verified contact lands directly in your existing sales workflow — no copy-pasting, no missed opportunities. Native connectors handle authentication and field mapping for you, and you can start exporting in a few clicks from the Integrations section in [app.skult.ai](https://app.skult.ai).

## Connect your CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In [app.skult.ai](https://app.skult.ai), click **Integrations** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your CRM">
    Choose your CRM from the list of available connectors. Each card shows the current connection status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Click **Connect** and complete the OAuth flow in the browser window that opens. Skult never stores your CRM password — authentication uses your CRM provider's standard OAuth 2.0 flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the connection">
    Once authorised, the connector card updates to **Connected**. You can disconnect or re-authenticate at any time from the same screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets synced

When you export from your inbox, Skult pushes the following data to your CRM:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contact information" icon="user">
    - Full name
    - Professional email address
    - Phone number
    - Job title and organisation
  </Card>
  <Card title="Signal data" icon="bell">
    - Signal score
    - Description and summary
    - Source (deliberation, budget, meeting minutes)
    - Detection date
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pipeline status" icon="chart-bar">
    - Current stage in your Skult pipeline
    - Priority tier (high / medium / low)
    - Notes attached to the alert
  </Card>
  <Card title="Source traceability" icon="file-lines">
    - Link back to the original public document
    - Municipality and department
    - Session date of the source document
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Export from your inbox

Your Skult inbox shows every detected buying signal as a card. Each card has a one-click export action that pushes both the signal data and the associated contact to your connected CRM.

1. Open the **Inbox** from the left sidebar.
2. Find the signal or contact card you want to export.
3. Click the **Export to CRM** button on the card.
4. Skult confirms the export with a success notification. The record appears in your CRM within seconds.

<Tip>
  You can export contacts independently of signals. Open a contact card and click **Export contact** to push only the contact record without the associated signal detail.
</Tip>

## Managing everything inside Skult

If you don't use an external CRM, Skult's built-in pipeline covers every step of your prospecting workflow:

- **Inbox** — review and score incoming signals
- **Pipeline view** — move alerts through custom stages (To Process, In Progress, Won, Lost…)
- **Contact profiles** — enriched decision-maker cards with contact details and signal history
- **Notes** — attach dated notes to any alert directly from the alert detail panel
- **Priority tiers** — flag alerts as high (tier 1), medium (tier 2), or low priority (tier 3)

No CRM connection is required to use any of these features.
